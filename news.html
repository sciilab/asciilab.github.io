June 2026

New Manuscript Approved to IEEE Sensor Letters

Our latest research titled "Multi-Condition Crack Detection and Surface Assessment for Underwater Metal Structures Using Adaptive Vision Models," spearheaded by PhD Researcher Md. Arif Khan and Sourav Roy, has been approved for early access. The paper details a novel methodology utilizing YOLOv8 and DenseNet-201 architectures for degraded aquatic environments.
