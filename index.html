Advancing Autonomous Frontiers

ASCII Lab conducts interdisciplinary research bridging advanced sensing techniques, intelligent robotics, computer vision, and cyber-physical technologies. We are dedicated to formulating robust solutions for real-world engineering challenges.

Our core focus encompasses the development of precision diagnostic tools and intelligent robotic systems specifically engineered for underwater inspection, structural health monitoring, and advanced autonomous navigation using state-of-the-art machine learning frameworks..

Mission

To engineer intelligent, reliable, and scalable sensing and control architectures for autonomous systems and diagnostic devices operating in complex environments.

Vision

To establish a world-class center of excellence driving indigenous innovation in advanced sensing techniques, intelligent mechatronics, and AI for India.

Research Domains

Pioneering advancements across interconnected disciplines to develop resilient cyber-physical systems.

Underwater Robotics

Development of Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROV) and AUVs for inspection, featuring subsea localization and structural health monitoring frameworks.

Computer Vision

Leveraging deep learning architectures (like YOLOv8 and DenseNet) for robust digital image processing, crack enumeration, and object detection in highly degraded visual environments.

Mechatronics & Control

Designing sophisticated embedded systems, intelligent control mechanisms, and autonomous navigation algorithms for UAVs and industrial automation.

Smart Sensing

Sensor fusion and non-destructive testing (NDT) approaches for structural evaluation, integrating IoT and FPGA systems for real-time edge processing.
